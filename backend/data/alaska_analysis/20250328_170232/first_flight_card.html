Is your child traveling alone?
Children under the age of 13 on Alaska Airlines flights must be linked to an adult reservation.  Please book the adult reservation first and have the confirmation code available to enter on the passenger information page.
Each infant must have their own ticket when traveling with our international partners. We've added your infant as an adult passenger; you'll be able to enter their correct birthdate later, on the Passenger Information page.
